The Beginning

It's noon and you're eating lunch at the local park.
Out of nowhere, a girl on a pink bicycle drops a package next to you as she quickly rides by. The package starts to ring.
Although upset at first, you become curious and begin to open it.

Inside the package is an Iphone, your passport, and what seems to be a pair of airplane tickets attached to a Post-It note. You answer the phone, and the recording of a familiar voice says "My life depends on you taking the right flight. You have one and a half hours to get to the DIA. Do not ask for help!" The phone call ends.

You feel yourself begin to tremble. You know this is for real, because that recording was the voice of the person you love most. Without hesitation, you grap your belongings and head for the lightrail. You know you can take the A line from the Colorado station all the way there, which is 35 minutes away. If you leave right now, you know you will barely make it. You read the note - it says "Paradise awaits."

Once on the lightrail, you look closely at the two plane tickets under your name. Both depart in 45 minutes, but to different destinations. One is to Cozumel, Mexico, and the other to San Juan, Puerto Rico. Now you must decide where you'll go.

You decide to go to Cozumel.
You decide to go to San Juan.
