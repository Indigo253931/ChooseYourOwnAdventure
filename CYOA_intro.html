by Monica Rodewald

The Beginning

It's almost noon and you're walking through a local park.
Out of nowhere, a girl on a bicycle drops a package next to you as she quickly rides by.


Although upset at first, you become curious and begin to open it.
The package begins to ring.
Inside you find a phone, a note, your passport, and what appear to be a pair of airplane tickets.



You answer the call, and a recording of a familiar voice says "My life depends on you taking the right flight.
You have 90 minutes to get to the DIA. Do not ask for help!"
The call ends.

You begin to tremble.
You know this is for real, because that was the voice of the person you love most.
Without hesitation, you grab your belongings and head for the lightrail.

You know you can take the A line all the way there.
If you leave now, you will barely make it.
You read the note - it says "Paradise awaits."

Once on the lightrail, you examine the two tickets under your name.
Both depart at the same time, but to different destinations.
One is destined for Cozumel, Mexico, and the other to San Juan, Puerto Rico.
Now you must make a choice.
